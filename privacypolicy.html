The Mathematical Proofs App does not request or require any permissions to access your device's data, camera, microphone, or any other feature. The App functions independently and does not interact with any other apps or services on your device.

The Mathematical Proofs App operates on a one-time purchase model. When you purchase the App, you gain access to all the content available within the App. Your purchase is associated with your device's app store account, but we do not have access to or store any of your payment information.

The Mathematical Proofs App does not integrate with or use any third-party services, libraries, or APIs that would result in the collection of your personal information.

The Mathematical Proofs App is not directed towards children under the age of 4. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If a parent or guardian becomes aware that their child has provided us with personal information, we encourage them to contact us, and we will take steps to remove such information from our systems.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes to the policy will be reflected in an updated version, and the effective date at the top of the policy will be modified accordingly. It is recommended that you review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
